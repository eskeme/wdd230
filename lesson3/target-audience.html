Target Audience

Two Personas

  • Persona 1: Maria Makiling is a 38 year-old mother who is seeking to improve his business by seeking more connection as well as increase the number of her clients within the baranggay. She uses the site to post her services as well as connect with business owners that she can collaborate with.
  • Persona 2: Genero Gembali is a 24 year old single guy who lives within the barangay who suddenly got his water shut off. He is wondering what happened so decided to google it and found information regarding a scheduled water interruption from our site. He now uses and checks the website regularly for any future events/happenings.

Five Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A local business owner would like to post regarding a special deal they would like to offer. The owner goes to contact us and find information on how to contact us along with our phone number for faster communication.
  • Scenario 2: A local would like to provide feedback regarding a service he/she received from a certain business. He/she finds out that the business information we have on our site is linked to their google page/facebook page and that's where they can add ratings/comments.
  • Scenario 3: A local business owner would like to use a fellow business owners service. He will find the updated business information for the said business.
  • Scenario 4: A business owner would like to new features be added to the website. He/she can find the information to contact with us directly by clicking the Contact Us button.
  • Scenario 5: One of the attendees of a particular event wants to see pictures taken from the event. He/she will find the images in our event section.
