Description of the target audience

The target audience is for all residents of the barangay. The website will be mobile friendly so that it can also cater to all ages including the elderly.

Two Personas

  • Residents of the barangay seeking information for random stuff happening like sudden black out or sudden water interruption.
  • Unemployed individuals looking for a job and residents looking for some services.
