Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to provide information to the residents of the barangay. A post will be made in the facebook page providing a link to the site with the information they are looking for. Examples of these informations are: Scheduled Power Interruptions, Scheduled Water Interruptions, Road Projects, Employment Opportunities, and events and other news.
